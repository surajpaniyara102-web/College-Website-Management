Course We Offer

We are offer tofour best computer science course for student by ITM Group Of Institute

B.Tech-(CSE)

The B.Tech CSE course is a full time Four years (Eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science And Engineering .The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Science And Engineering.

B.Tech(CSE-DS)

The B.Tech(Data Scientist) in DS is basically about storing, processing, securing, and managing information. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

B.Tech-(CE)

The B.Tech in (CE) is an undergraduate degree course in Computer Science And Engineering for a duration of 4 years. The field relates to design, development and use of computer applications.

B.Tech-(ME)

The B.Tech ME course is a full time Four years (Eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering .The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Mechanical Engineering.

B.Tech(CSE-AI)

B.Tech(CSE-AI & ML)

B.Tech(ECE)

The B.Tech(Electronics and Communication Engineering) Electronics and Communication Engineering A branch of engineering that focuses on the application of electronics and communication systems to design and manufacture devices and networks. ECE engineers work on a variety of projects, including designing and maintaining satellites, developing communication facilities, and creating programs for control and communication systems

M.Tech(CSE)

The B.Tech(Computer Science And Engineering) An M.Tech in CSE can help students gain specialized skills in the computer science field. It can also help students develop the ability to conduct research in the field.

BBA

BBA stands for Bachelor of Business Administration, a bachelor's degree that provides students with the skills and knowledge to work in business management. BBA programs are designed to help students understand business operations and prepare them for real-world business challenges.

BCA

BCA full form is a Bachelor of Computer Applications.It's a 3-year degree that equips you to build software applications.It covers programming languages like C, C++, and Java, along with database management, networking, web development, software engineering, data structures, and computer architecture.

DIPLOMA-(ME)

About Us

The ITM GROUP OF INSTITUTE at Maharajganj provide one of the earliest form of technical studies, which has been vital in setting up the standard of brilliance. About-ITM Founded in 2009, ITM College, Maharajganj, has established itself as a prominent center for higher education in the region. Our journey began with a vision to provide quality education and foster a learning environment that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth among our students. Over the years, ITM College has grown into a multidisciplinary institution, offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs that cater to the diverse educational needs of the community. We have dedicated ourselves to empowering our students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in an ever-evolving global environment. Our campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, and dedicated faculty members who bring their expertise and passion into the classroom. At ITM College, we strive to create a dynamic and interactive learning experience, ensuring that our students are prepared to meet the challenges of the future.
